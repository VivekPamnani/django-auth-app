Instructions

Note that you can access this page at any time by clicking the 'Instructions' button on the top-right of your screen on your home page.

General instructions:
  1. You must participate in this study using a laptop or a desktop.
  2. This study requires you to have a real keyboard.
  3. This study comprises of 6 sessions. (You have the right to stop participating at any point in time).
In the first session:

The study will last for approximately 45 minutes and consists of three parts:

  1. Survey about your demographic information
  2. A sequence of 3 mini-games to play.
  3. Health questionnaires.
In a subsequent session (i.e., session two to session six):

The study will last for approximately 35 minutes and consists of only two parts:

  1. The 3 mini-games and,
  2. Health questionnaires.
Directions to keep in mind:
  1. Kindly refrain from using mobiles/tablets/iPads for the study.
  2. Make sure you are in a quiet environment and free from distractions.
  3. Please sit straight during the course of the study.
  4. Adjust the monitor/display so that the top of the screen is at—or slightly below—eye level.
  5. Your eyes should look slightly downward when viewing the middle of the screen.
  6. Position the monitor from your eyes at about an arm's length distance (at least 20 inches or 51 cm). If your screen is larger, add more viewing distance.

If you have any questions regarding the instructions or if you need any kind of clarification, please write an email addressed to : vivek.pamnani@research.iiit.ac.in (research student), or priyanka.srivastava@iiit.ac.in (principal investigator) with 'IMWBS' as part of the subject.

Further, if at any point of time you need refer the instructions again, enter the following URL in your browser and hit enter: https://imwbs.pythonanywhere.com/user/instructions/. You can also go to the home page of our website and click the appropriate button (on top-right) that will redirect you to the same webpage.
