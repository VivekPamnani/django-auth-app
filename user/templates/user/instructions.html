Instructions

Note that you can access this page at any time by clicking the 'Instructions' button on the top-right of your screen on your home page.

General Instructions/Procedure
  1. Click the 'Proceed' button on the dashboard to proceed to the test.
  2. Before you are redirected to the study website, you will be presented a validation code that you should note.
  3. Once you confirm, you will be redirected to the experiment website.
  4. Instructions for the study will be addressed there as you go through your session.
  5. At the end of your session, you will be asked to enter the validation code you received earlier on this website.
  6. You will be asked for your UPI ID when you complete the survey, via which you will be receiving your compensation. Note that this is only so that we can pay you.
  7. Logout from the website and please visit the site again in two weeks. You will receive an email reminder for the same.
If this is your first session:

The study will last for approximately 45 minutes and consists of four parts:

  1. Survey about your demographic information
  2. A sequence of mini-games to play.
  3. Further questions related to the study.
  4. Health questionnaires.
If this is NOT your first session:

The study will last for approximately 35 minutes and consists of two parts:

  • The mini-games and,
  • Health questionnaires.
Directions to keep in mind.
  1. This study requires you to be using a laptop or a desktop. Kindly refrain from using mobiles/tablets/iPads for the study.
  2. Make sure you are in a quiet environment and free from distractions.
  3. Please sit straight during the course of the study.
  4. The computer screen should ideally be at or slightly above your eye level.
  5. If the directions 3 and 4 make you uncomfortable, please adjust accordingly so that you feel comfortable. That is most important.

If you have any questions regarding the instructions or if you need any kind of clarification, please write an email addressed to : vivek.pamnani@research.iiit.ac.in (research student), or priyanka.srivastava@iiit.ac.in (principal investigator) with 'IMWBS' as part of the subject.

Further, if at any point of time you need refer the instructions again, enter the following URL in your browser and hit enter: https://imwbs.pythonanywhere.com/user/instructions/. You can also go to the home page of our website and click the appropriate button (on top-right) that will redirect you to the same webpage.

If you have understood the above directions, please confirm by clicking the button below to proceed.
